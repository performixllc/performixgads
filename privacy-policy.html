Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Your privacy matters. AuditX collects only the information necessary to deliver our Google Ads audit and competitor intelligence services. We do not sell your data, ever.

1. Who We Are

AuditX is a Google Ads audit and competitor intelligence service operated by Performix LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). We provide advertising account audits, competitor analysis, and strategic recommendations to businesses and marketing teams.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: hello@auditx.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Booking information — When you book a call via Cal.com, we receive your name, email address, and any details you provide in the booking form.
  • Business information — During our discovery call and audit process, you may share your website URL, Google Ads account details, ad spend, and business goals.
  • Communication data — Emails, messages, or documents exchanged during the service delivery.
  • Payment information — We collect bank transfer details only for the purpose of processing ACH/wire payments. We do not store card details.
  • Usage data — Basic analytics about how visitors interact with this website (page views, session duration, referral source).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and improve our audit and competitor intelligence services
  • Communicate with you about your project, deliverables, and follow-ups
  • Process payments for services rendered
  • Send service-related updates (not marketing spam)
  • Improve our website and service quality
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only with:

  • Service providers — Tools we use to operate (e.g., Cal.com for scheduling, Google Workspace for communication). These providers are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Legal compliance — If required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers — In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may transfer to the new entity under equivalent privacy protections.

Your Google Ads account data is used solely to perform your audit and is never shared with third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected:

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us. We will action requests within 30 days.

This website may use basic cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use our services.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@auditx.com. We will respond within 30 days.

We recommend that you do not share sensitive credentials (e.g., Google Ads account passwords) via email. Where access is required, we recommend using Google Ads Manager access invitations.
